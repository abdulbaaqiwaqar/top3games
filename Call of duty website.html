What is Call of Duty?

Call of Duty is a computer game that revolutionised the first-person shooter genre, receiving multiple game of the year awards in 2003 and 2004 after its first release in 2003. Call of Duty was developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. It was set during World War II and let players to experience the conflict through the eyes of American, Soviet, and British soldiers.
